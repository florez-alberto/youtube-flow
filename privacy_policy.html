Privacy Policy

YouTube Flow

This privacy policy describes how the YouTube Flow browser extension handles your data.

Summary

We believe in your right to privacy. YouTube Flow does not collect, store, share, or transmit any of your personal data. The extension operates entirely locally on your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, browsing history, or usage data. The extension's sole purpose is to interact with the YouTube player interface to prevent playback pauses.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • Host Permissions (`*://*.youtube.com/*`): This is strictly necessary to inject the script that detects and clicks the "Continue watching" prompt on YouTube video pages.

This permission is used solely for the functionality of the extension and not for data collection.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking scripts, or external servers. No data is sent out of your browser.

Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
