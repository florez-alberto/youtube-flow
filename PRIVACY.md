# Privacy Policy

**Extension Name**: YouTube Flow  
**Developer**: Alberto Florez  
**Effective Date**: January 12, 2026

## Introduction
This privacy policy describes how the **YouTube Flow** browser extension handles your data.

## Data Collection
We believe in your right to privacy. **YouTube Flow does not collect, store, share, or transmit any of your personal data.** 

The extension operates entirely locally on your device. We do not track your browsing history, collect usage metrics, or store any personal identifiable information.

## Permissions
The extension requires the following permissions to function:

- **Host Permissions (`*://*.youtube.com/*`)**: This is strictly necessary to inject the content script that detects and automatically clicks the "Continue watching" prompt on YouTube video pages.

This permission is used solely for the core functionality of the extension and not for any form of data collection or surveillance.

## Third-Party Services
This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking scripts, or external servers. No data is sent out of your browser.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/florez-alberto/youtube-flow).
